- Typography -

It is the art and technique of arranging type to make written language legible, readable and appealing when displayed.

Popular font-family style:

  1. It is serif style.
  2. It is sans-serif style.

Popular font-sizes:

  • For normal text we use 16px - 32px and less than 400 bold!
  • For long text(like a blog post) we use the 20px!
  • For headline we use more than 50px and bold 600!
  • For per line we use less than 75 characters!
  • For line height we use 1.5 or 2. !
